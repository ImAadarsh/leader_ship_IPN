About The Summit

About School Education 5.0: IPN Leadership Summit 2025

The IPN Leadership Summit 2025 is dedicated to pioneering the vision of School Education 5.0, a transformative framework that redefines learning by integrating advanced technologies, sustainability-focused methodologies, and collaborative approaches.

This summit will convene education leaders, policymakers, industry experts, and thought leaders to shape a roadmap for the future of school education. Building on the success of previous editions, IPN Leadership Summit 2025 will adopt an inclusive and forward-thinking approach by hosting six nationwide dialogues in key cities across India.

School Education 5.0 Framework

  • Advanced technology in school education
  • Sustainability-focused learning
  • Personalized education methodologies
  • Industry collaboration for skill development
  • AI-driven learning ecosystems
  • Global citizenship and environmental consciousness

Key Highlights

Key Highlights of the Summit

Six Nationwide Dialogues

The IPN Leadership Summit 2025 will host six strategic dialogues across India in different cities, each focusing on advancing School Education 5.0. These dialogues will engage local and national stakeholders in meaningful discussions.

Engaging Key Stakeholders

The summit will bring together school leaders, policymakers, corporate partners, ed-tech innovators, researchers, and students to foster collaboration and drive systemic change in School Education 5.0.

Interactive Panel Discussions

Renowned educationists, technology leaders, and policymakers will participate in high-impact conversations to share best practices and innovative ideas on topics such as AI in education and personalized learning pathways.

Next-Gen Ed-Tech Innovations

Live demonstrations of AI-driven learning tools, VR/AR applications, robotics, and sustainability-driven solutions will highlight how schools can adopt cutting-edge technologies aligned with School Education 5.0.

School Education 5.0 is a transformative framework that redefines learning by integrating advanced technology, sustainability-focused learning, personalized education methodologies, industry collaboration for skill development, AI-driven learning ecosystems, and global citizenship principles into educational systems.
